About Savanna Bites

Discover more about us

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Ms.Nampuuma Sumayia, Savanna Bites has been serving delicious African and Continental cuisine to the community for about 5five(5) years. What started as a small individually-owned restaurant has grown into a beloved dining destination.

Our passion is to bring the rich flavors and traditions of African cuisine to every plate. We source ingredients directly from trusted suppliers globally who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Every dish tells a story of heritage, culture, and love. We invite you to be part of our family and experience the warmth of African hospitality.

Mission

To serve authentic, high-quality African cuisine while creating a welcoming environment where families and friends can gather and celebrate together.

Our Values

  • 1.Authenticity in every dish
  • 2.Quality ingredients always
  • 3.Exceptional customer service
  • 4.Community and tradition
